# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Product Overview

AutoStream is a cloud-based video streaming and content management platform designed for content creators, educators, and businesses. It enables users to upload, host, manage, and stream videos to audiences worldwide with professional-grade quality. AutoStream also offers AI-powered tools to help creators grow faster.

---

## Plans & Pricing

### Basic Plan — $29/month

The Basic Plan is designed for independent creators just starting out.

- **Price:** $29 per month
- **Video uploads:** Up to 10 videos per month
- **Maximum resolution:** 720p HD
- **Storage:** 50 GB included
- **Bandwidth:** 200 GB/month
- **Analytics:** Basic view counts and watch time
- **Support:** Email support (response within 48 hours)
- **AI Captions:** Not included
- **4K Streaming:** Not available on Basic Plan
- **Custom domain:** Not included
- **Team members:** 1 seat (solo creator)

### Pro Plan — $79/month

The Pro Plan is built for professional creators who need unlimited access and advanced features.

- **Price:** $79 per month
- **Video uploads:** Unlimited videos per month
- **Maximum resolution:** 4K Ultra HD
- **Storage:** 500 GB included
- **Bandwidth:** Unlimited
- **Analytics:** Advanced analytics with audience insights, heatmaps, and engagement metrics
- **Support:** 24/7 priority support via live chat and phone
- **AI Captions:** Included — automatic caption generation in 30+ languages
- **4K Streaming:** Available on Pro Plan
- **Custom domain:** Included
- **Team members:** Up to 5 seats
- **Ad-free viewing:** Guaranteed for your audience

## Refund Policy

AutoStream offers a **7-day money-back guarantee** for new subscribers.

- Refund requests submitted within 7 days of the initial subscription purchase will be honored in full.
- **No refunds are issued after 7 days** from the subscription start date.
- Refunds do not apply to partial months, mid-cycle downgrades, or add-on purchases.
- To request a refund within the eligible window, contact support at billing@autostream.io with your account email and reason for cancellation.
- Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days back to the original payment method.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Can I upload videos longer than 2 hours on the Basic Plan?**
A: Yes, the Basic Plan does not impose a per-video length limit. However, each video counts toward your 10-video monthly upload limit, and your total storage is capped at 50 GB.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my monthly video upload limit on the Basic Plan?**
A: If you hit your 10-video upload limit on Basic, you will not be able to upload additional videos until the next billing cycle begins, or until you upgrade to the Pro Plan, which has unlimited uploads.

**Q: Does AutoStream support 4K video streaming?**
A: 4K Ultra HD streaming is exclusively available on the Pro Plan at $79/month. The Basic Plan supports video up to 720p HD resolution. If you upload a 4K video on the Basic Plan, it will be automatically downscaled to 720p.

**Q: How does the AI captions feature work on the Pro Plan?**
A: On the Pro Plan, AutoStream automatically generates captions for your videos using AI. Captions are available in 30+ languages and can be edited within the platform. They are ready within minutes of video upload and can be toggled on or off by viewers.

**Q: Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro mid-month?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade at any time. When you upgrade mid-cycle, you will be charged a prorated amount for the remaining days in your billing cycle. Your account will immediately reflect all Pro Plan features upon upgrade.

**Q: Is there a free trial available?**
A: AutoStream does not currently offer a free trial. However, all new subscriptions come with a 7-day money-back guarantee, which gives you risk-free access to try the platform fully.

**Q: Can I host live streams on AutoStream?**
A: Live streaming is a feature available exclusively to Pro Plan subscribers. It supports streams up to 4K resolution and includes a live chat overlay for audience engagement. Basic Plan subscribers cannot host live streams.

**Q: How do I cancel my AutoStream subscription?**
A: You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Account Settings under the Billing section. After cancellation, your account remains active until the end of the current billing period. AutoStream does not offer refunds for cancellations made after the 7-day money-back window.
